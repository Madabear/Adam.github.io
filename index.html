Web Design

takegoodcare.

Take Good Care are a new health and wellbeing business based in Lewes, East Sussex. Fed up with using multiple online retailers, the owner wanted to create a single point of purchase for all things health and wellbeing.

I designed and built an ecommerce website using the popular platforms WordPress and WooCommerce in order for them to sell their products online. The design focuses on upselling popular and related items in order to maximise sales to their target audience.

View the live site here: takegoodcare.co.uk

Web Design

Arden Surgical

Arden Surgical are a new medical aesthetics business based near Birmingham. I was tasked with designing a new website to help launch the business and promote their services.

From Concept to Build

The initial design was planned out and mocked up using design tool Canva. Creating the general look and feel of the website, focusing specifically on layout, colours, images was an important step to ensure the client was properly briefed. Allowing the client to sign off on initial design helped to speed up the build and review processes.

View the live site here: ardensurgical.co.uk

Web Design

The English Coach

The English Coach is a tutoring based on English skills. They provide business owners and professionals the opportunity to improve their English speaking skills.

They had an existing website which they were paying high monthly fees for. In addition to this, they were not given access to make edits or changes themselves. I was tasked with recreating their website using the content management system, WordPress. Using a popular and robust WordPress theme I successfully rebuilt their website making minor improvements in addition. Afterward I provided training on how to use and update the website so they could update it themselves.

View the live site here: theenglishcoach.biz
